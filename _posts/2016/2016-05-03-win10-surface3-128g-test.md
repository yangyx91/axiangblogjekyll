伴随着微软终止Windows RT 8.1重大更新计划，Surface RT/Surface 2两代平板也被迫停产和退役，笔者在出手Surface 2后，再次海淘一款贫民版Win10平板玩具：10寸屏美版Surface3，默认为4G内存+128G存储方案。

<a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface9" rel="attachment wp-att-9478" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9478" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface9.jpg" alt="ebay-surface9" width="450" height="351" /></a>本次海淘来自ebay，Surface3平板九成新，默认附带了Surface蓝色键盘，整个流程历时22天左右，关税也在410元，不过还是比国行Surface3便宜了2K元，依旧划算。

<a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface1" rel="attachment wp-att-9470" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9470" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface1.jpg" alt="ebay-surface1" width="500" height="163" /></a>默认配置方面，Surface3平板采用了英特尔Atom x7 四核心处理器，10寸1080P显示屏，搭载了64位Windows 8.1家庭版，可完整升级到Win10体验。

机器整体大约620g，比Surface2更轻便，延续了经典Surface支架设计，可调整3个角度，满足日常各种使用环境。

<a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface4" rel="attachment wp-att-9473" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9472" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface3.jpg" alt="ebay-surface3" width="450" height="256" /><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9473" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface4.jpg" alt="ebay-surface4" width="450" height="266" /></a>在接口方面，Surface3继续保持了一个USB 3.0接口、DP显示输出口，同时首次采用micro-usb充电方案，经典Windows徽标也移动到屏幕右侧；

<a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface6" rel="attachment wp-att-9475" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9475" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface6.jpg" alt="ebay-surface6" width="450" height="253" /></a>小插曲：美国卖家还特意为Surface3送了2年版Eset Nod32反病毒安全软件，安全、轻量两不误。

<a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface5" rel="attachment wp-att-9474" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9474" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface5.jpg" alt="ebay-surface5" width="450" height="258" /></a>续航方面，Surface3输于Surface RT/Surface2，强于Surface Pro 3，笔者实际测试，Surface3在Wifi上网、720P视频播放情况下，可以坚持6个小时；飞行模式下，可以坚持8.5小时，续航还是非常可观的。

<a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface7" rel="attachment wp-att-9476" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9476" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface7.jpg" alt="ebay-surface7" width="450" height="284" /></a><a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface8" rel="attachment wp-att-9477" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9477" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface8.jpg" alt="ebay-surface8" width="450" height="259" /></a>当Surface3升级上Win10后，我们可以用上“你好小娜”语音唤醒、Edge浏览器以及今年7月推送的“Win10周年更新”，配合Windows Ink触控笔技术，值得一试。

<a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface10" rel="attachment wp-att-9479" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9479" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface10.jpg" alt="ebay-surface10" width="300" height="498" /></a>

<a href="http://www.axiangblog.com/win10-surface3-128g-test.html/ebay-surface2" rel="attachment wp-att-9471" target="_blank"  rel="nofollow" ><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9471" src="http://www.axiangblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/ebay-surface2.jpg" alt="ebay-surface2" width="450" height="253" /></a>最后不得不提下Surface3的Tpye Cover原装键盘，可选黑色、蓝色等色彩，采用了磁铁扣设计，贴合Surface3平板非常牢固，值得入手，辅助一支Surface3触控笔，疗效更好。
